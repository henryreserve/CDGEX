About HNReserve

HNReserve is a privately operated digital asset management group focused on cryptocurrency, equity, and gold markets. The operation is built around disciplined execution, capital protection, and controlled risk exposure rather than speculative return targets.

Investment Mandate

HNReserve operates a discretionary trading mandate. Capital is deployed only when market structure, liquidity conditions, and risk parameters align. No trades are forced, and cash is considered a valid position.

Risk Management Framework

Capital Preservation

Protection of capital takes priority over return generation. Risk is controlled before opportunity is considered.

Position Sizing

Exposure is scaled based on volatility, liquidity, and market conditions. No single position dominates overall risk.

Drawdown Awareness

Risk is reduced during unfavorable conditions. Activity is adjusted to preserve capital during drawdown periods.
